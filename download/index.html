Stable

GeoServer 2.28.2

Recommended GeoServer for production use,
tested and supported by the community.

The 2.28 series is scheduled for six-months of
updates, providing improvements and bug fixes.

GeoServer stable release:

    • 2.28.2

Nightly builds for the 2.28.x series can be found here.

Maintenance

GeoServer 2.27.5

Support update for existing installations,
providing you a chance to upgrade.

The prior 2.27 series remains available, scheduled
for six-months of minor updates, for bug fixes.

GeoServer 2.27 maintenance release:

    • 2.27.5

Nightly builds for the 2.27.x series can be found here.
